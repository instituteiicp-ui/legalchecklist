Part 6: Critical Legal Updates (2025)

1. The "50% CTC" Rule (Code on Wages)

The Rule: Allowances cannot exceed 50% of the Total CTC. Basic Pay must be at least 50%.
Impact: If your Basic is low, you must increase it. This increases the PF liability for both employer and employee.

2. Aadhaar-UAN Linking Mandatory

Employers can no longer file ECR (PF Return) for employees whose Aadhaar is not linked and verified with their UAN. Ensure 100% seeding.
